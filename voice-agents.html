// HOW IT WORKS

Three phases from kickoff to live.

01

We learn your operation

We map your existing call flows, scripts, FAQs, and escalation paths. Nothing is invented — the agent reflects how your business actually works.

02

We build and train the agent

Your AI voice agent is configured, tested, and refined against real scenarios. We tune tone, pacing, and language until it sounds like your brand.

03

It goes live — and keeps learning

The agent handles real calls from day one. We monitor performance, review edge cases, and update flows so it gets sharper over time.

// CAPABILITIES

What your agent handles.

Inbound call handling

Answers every call immediately, gathers context, routes appropriately, and resolves common queries without transferring to a human.

Lead qualification

Asks the right questions, scores the lead, and delivers a qualified summary to your CRM or team — before a human ever gets involved.

Appointment scheduling

Books, confirms, and reschedules appointments in real time — synced to your calendar without any back-and-forth.

FAQ & support queries

Handles your most common questions — pricing, hours, service details — so your team isn't answering the same thing 40 times a day.

Outbound follow-up

Proactively contacts leads, follows up on quotes, and confirms appointments — at scale, without adding a single person to payroll.

Seamless escalation

When a situation needs a human, the agent hands off with full context — no caller has to repeat themselves.
