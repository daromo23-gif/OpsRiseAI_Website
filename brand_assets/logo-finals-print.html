Final delivery — concepts 02 Slash + 04 Bracket.

Each concept ships in three forms: the full lockup (horizontal, for headers and signatures), the isolated mark (square, for app icons and avatars), and three color variants (Ink, Cream, Ember). Specs at the bottom of each section cover clearspace, minimum size, and don'ts.

Construction

  • Family: Space Mono Bold, 700 wt
  • Tracking: 0.06em
  • Two-line lockup, baseline offset 2.6em
  • Ember (#D85A2B) on //, ↗, AI only
  • Body of name inherits surface fg

Clearspace · Min size · Don'ts

  • Clearspace: 1× the "O" on every side
  • Min lockup width: 120 px (digital), 32 mm (print)
  • Min isolated mark: 16 px
  • Never recolor //↗ outside Ember / Ink / Cream
  • Never break the two-line stack — it's the trademark
